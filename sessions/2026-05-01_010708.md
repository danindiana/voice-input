### Phase 2: cpal Audio Validation

**Key finding: cpal device naming via ALSA**
- cpal's ALSA backend does NOT expose `alsa_input.usb-UC03_UC03-00.mono-fallback` by name
- The correct device is `"pipewire"` — PipeWire's ALSA plugin, routes to WirePlumber default source (UC03)
- 32 kHz mono I16 confirmed working
- Installed `libxdo-dev` for enigo

**audio-test binary** (`src/bin/audio_test.rs`):
- Enumerates ALSA devices, captures 5s at 32 kHz from "pipewire" device
- Validated: 159,715 samples, RMS 0.0135, saved to `/tmp/voice-cpal-test.wav`
